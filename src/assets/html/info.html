Things we check

In 14 years in business, our auditors have studied over 2 500 hotel websites to reveal what affects website conversion into bookings.

Ranking in search engines

Your website’s ranking directly affects the number of website visitors. That is why we look for reasons that prevent the website from getting to the top of search results.

Website navigation

We check if the navigation menu of the website is clear and easy for searching. It should contain all the necessary menu sections that shall be laid out in the way most familiar for users.

Content

The website should have all the information that travelers might be looking for when they search for accommodation. We check if your hotel website has it all, and it is easy to perceive.

Marketing and converting features

The converting hotel website encourages users to book. This means that it has pricing information, Call-to-Action buttons, and purchase motivators all in the right places.
